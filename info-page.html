FolusBudget Info

Hello and welcome to the info page of FolusBudget. Here you can budget your periodic expenses.

How to access FolusBudget

FolusBudget is runnable on any operating system.

If you want to access the website through an external server you can simply go to the link folusbudget.com and ensure there are no websites on your blacklist.

If you want to run it locally you can download the source code after purchasing the licencse from folusbudgetsourcecode@github.com. In summary, the requirements are flask, some python modules and internet connection to access the style sheet. You can just follow the manual in the link to install.

Features of FolusBudget

You can add catgories and the percentage of your money you want to spend on this category.


You can view your budget.

You can change the percentage of an existing category.

You can search your budget for the amount you can spend on a category.
